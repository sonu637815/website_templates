Travel Tips

Packing & Travel Guides

Expert advice to make every journey smoother, safer, and more memorable.

01

Pack Light, Travel Far

Stick to a carry-on — 10 essential items cover 90% of any trip. Roll don't fold, and always bring a universal adapter.

02

Health & Vaccinations

Check CDC recommendations 6–8 weeks before departure. Carry a basic kit with rehydration salts and any prescriptions.

03

Digital Backup Everything

Scan your passport, insurance, and bookings. Save to Google Drive or email yourself — losing docs abroad is avoidable.

04

Local Currency & ATMs

Withdraw cash at airport ATMs on arrival. Notify your bank before travel and always keep a backup emergency card.

05

Respect Local Culture

Learn a few phrases, dress modestly at religious sites, and ask before photographing people. Thoughtfulness opens doors.

06

Travel Insurance Is Non-Negotiable

A single hospital visit abroad can cost tens of thousands. Always insure for medical evacuation and trip cancellation.
